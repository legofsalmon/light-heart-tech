About Lightheart

Lightheart is Dublin's first purpose-built immersive gallery, opening September 2026 on the city's quays. The venue features 14 4K laser projectors, an L-ISA spatial audio system with up to 50 speakers, and a quarterly changing exhibition programme that combines international and Irish artists with world-class technology.

Lightheart represents an investment of approximately €4–4.5 million and is positioned as cultural infrastructure — a permanent addition to Dublin's art landscape that raises the national standard for immersive art.

Story Angles

Six ways to cover Lightheart

Cultural Long-Form

A New Kind of Cultural Space

Lightheart is Ireland's first purpose-built immersive gallery — a permanent, curated space that changes completely every quarter. Why now, why Dublin, and what does it mean for Ireland's cultural landscape?

Targets: Irish Times Arts, Journal of Music, Totally Dublin, Frieze

Technology / Innovation

The Tech Behind the Art

Behind the art is world-class technical infrastructure — 14 4K laser projectors, L-ISA spatial audio from L-Acoustics, and full surface mapping. A deep dive into the technology enabling immersive art at this scale.

Targets: Silicon Republic, Tech Central, Wired UK

Tourism & Lifestyle

Dublin's Newest Must-Visit Destination

A new reason to visit Dublin — a quarterly-changing immersive gallery that offers an experience unlike anything in Ireland. Positioned between the Guinness Storehouse and the city centre.

Targets: Irish Independent Travel, Lonely Planet, Fáilte Ireland

Founder / Business

From NCAD to €4.5 Million Gallery

The founder's journey from art school to purchasing buildings on the quays and investing €4.5 million in a vision that didn't exist in Ireland. Cultural entrepreneurship and filling a gap nobody else would fill.

Targets: Business Post, Irish Examiner, Forbes

Artist Feature

Meet the First Artist

The first artist to activate Dublin's new immersive gallery on creating work for a space unlike any other in Ireland, and what it means to bring Lightheart's rooms to life.

Targets: Visual Artists Ireland, Paper Visual Art, Artforum

Architecture & Design

Transforming the Quays

The architectural transformation — from purchased buildings to purpose-built immersive rooms. The design decisions, technical challenges, and what it takes to build a space where every surface becomes a canvas.

Targets: Architecture Ireland, Dezeen, Wallpaper*
