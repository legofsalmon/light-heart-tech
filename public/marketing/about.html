A space Dublin has been waiting for.

Lightheart exists because we believe that art should be felt, not just observed. That Dublin deserves a world-class cultural space. And that technology, when it serves beauty, can restore the kind of wonder that stops thought and resets the nervous system.

Why We Exist

Closing the gap between you and art

For too long, art in Dublin has been something you stand in front of. Lightheart closes that gap permanently.

Here, art is not on a wall but in the air around you. In the sound moving through your body. In the light that shifts when you shift. We exist because Dublin deserves a space where collective wonder isn't a memory from childhood but something that happens every quarter, in a purpose-built room, at the frontier of what technology and art can achieve together.

What We Believe

Four principles that guide everything

Independence

Our programme is shaped by curatorial instinct and artistic ambition — not by what is safe, expected, or proven elsewhere. This independence is what makes our voice meaningful.

Future Optimism

The work shown at Lightheart reflects a belief that art and technology are in conversation, not in tension. We programme artists who use new tools without losing sight of the human experience at the centre of all art-making.

Awe

Awe is not spectacle. Spectacle overwhelms; awe expands. The works we present have the capacity to make a visitor feel, for a moment, that the world is larger than they thought. This is the highest ambition for a public art institution.

Pluralism

The audience for Lightheart is everyone — those who visit galleries regularly and those who never have. A genuinely pluralist programme does not condescend to either group.

The Space

Purpose-built for immersion

A purpose-built immersive gallery on Dublin's quays, near the Guinness Storehouse. Two projection rooms. 14 4K laser projectors mapping every surface. An L-ISA spatial audio system with up to 50 speakers creating 360-degree sound. Floors, walls, and architectural elements all become part of the canvas.

The technology is world-class. But you won't think about the technology. You'll think about how it makes you feel.

The Vision

Two layers. One purpose.

Layer 1 — For visitors

A world-class immersive gallery with quarterly changing exhibitions. Every visit is a new world.

Layer 2 — For Ireland

Community infrastructure for digital art. A platform that proves Ireland can sustain world-class cultural innovation. A space where artists build careers at home, where education and industry intersect, and where the national conversation about art and technology advances.

Layer 1 always leads. Layer 2 enriches.
