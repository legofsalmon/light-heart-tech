FAQ

Common questions

What happens if I don't renew?

Your founding designation on the wall remains — permanently. Active benefits cease, but your place in Lightheart's history is secure. If you wish to rejoin later, you can at the then-current rate.

Can I upgrade my tier later?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. The price difference will be calculated pro-rata for your current membership year.

Is this tax deductible?

Founding Circle memberships may be tax deductible for some members. Consult your tax advisor. We can provide receipts and documentation as needed.

When will I receive my welcome pack?

Welcome packs will be dispatched in August 2026, ahead of the September gallery opening. This allows us to include exhibition-specific materials and ensures everything meets our quality standards.
