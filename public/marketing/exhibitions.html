A new world every quarter.

Every three months, Lightheart transforms completely. New artists. New themes. New technology. The space you visited last time no longer exists — and neither does the one that comes next.

How It Works

From arrival to transformation

1

A New Exhibition Opens

Each exhibition runs for approximately 12 weeks. We work with international and Irish artists to create experiences that could only exist in this space.

2

You Choose Your Session

Each session accommodates a limited number of visitors. The experience lasts approximately 50 minutes. We cap capacity because the experience matters more than the throughput.

3

You Arrive, Enter, Feel

From the moment you step inside, the outside world disappears. No guided tour. No audio guide. Just you, the art, and the people beside you.

4

You Leave Changed

The best experiences don't end when you walk out. They stay with you. They become something you describe to friends and return for next quarter.

The Quarterly Difference

Why we change everything every three months

Most immersive experiences stay the same. Van Gogh shows tour for years with the same content. Illusion galleries never change. This creates a single-visit mindset: been there, done that.

Lightheart's quarterly model transforms the gallery into a cultural rhythm. Each exhibition is a new reason to visit, a new conversation to have, a new experience to share. It creates membership value. It gives the media something to write about four times a year. And it lets us programme ambitiously — taking risks, responding to the world, and keeping our community engaged.
