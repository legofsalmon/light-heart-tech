Triple Stack Model — Operational Checklists

The TSM audits readiness across three layers. Every exhibition, every event, every visitor experience is checked against these stacks. Phase 1 focuses on the Global Stack (infrastructure). The Internal and External Stacks become primary in Phases 2-3.

Global Stack

Infrastructure and systems — what must be true before anyone walks in
Ground◐ Electrical, HVAC, safety
Runtime○ Media server, clock sync
Circulation○ Visitor flow, entry sequence
Channels○ GDPR, consent, recording
Frames○ Sightlines, luminance
Roles○ Staff positions, training
Horizons○ Quarterly review cycle

Internal Stack

Embodied perception — what visitors feel in their body
Root○ Floor surface, seating, temp
Sacral○ Pacing, emotional arc
Solar Plexus○ Choice architecture
Heart○ Group pacing, co-regulation
Throat○ Staff language, signage
Third Eye○ Attention design, gaze
Crown○ Exit, reflection, integration

External Stack

Transition and relational space — how visitors move in and out
Space○ Sightline, route mapping
Portal○ Entry count-in, consent
Gesture○ Movement pacing, abort
Mirror○ Feedback, echo design
Narrative○ Journey arc, offboarding
Atmosphere○ Luminance, SPL caps
Feedback Loop○ Learning cycles, data
Concrescence — The moment all three stacks align. Opening night is the first. Each quarterly exhibition opening is another.

Three-Question Decision Rule

Every major decision passes through these three filters before execution:

Before any significant decision, ask:

  1. What would Jiwa do? — Does this align with our story? Does it lead with how the visitor feels?
  2. What would Sutherland test? — Is there a counter-intuitive option we're ignoring? What's the psycho-logic angle?
  3. What would Godin make remarkable? — Would a sneezer share this? Does it avoid the trap of invisibility?

Morning Standup (5 min, daily)

Each team member answers the three questions above for their current work. No lengthy updates — just the three filters applied to today's priorities.

Weekly Pulse Check (30 min)

Full three-question framework review across all active workstreams. Blocker check. Commitments for the next week.
