What We Are

A space where art and technology meet

Lightheart is a purpose-built immersive gallery on Dublin's quays. Every quarter, we transform our space into a completely new world — large-scale projections across every surface, spatial audio that moves through the room, and art that you don't stand in front of. You stand inside it.

This is not a selfie museum. This is not a theme park. This is a space where art and technology meet to create the kind of collective wonder that makes a room full of strangers forget to check their phones.

Opening September 2026.

◈
The Experience

Three elements. One transformation.

◉

Total Immersion

14 4K laser projectors map every surface — walls, floors, architectural details. The boundary between you and the art dissolves. You're not watching. You're inside.

◎

Sound That Moves

Our L-ISA spatial audio system places sound in three-dimensional space. Music doesn't play from speakers — it travels through the room, above you, beside you, through you.

◈

A New World Every Quarter

Every three months, a completely new exhibition. New artists. New themes. New feelings. Each visit is a reason to return — and a reason to bring someone who hasn't been.

You just have to go.

We're designing experiences that resist easy description. The kind of thing you try to explain at dinner and end up saying: you just have to be there. That's not a failure of language. That's how you know it worked.
