Technical System at a Glance

Two immersive rooms, ~280 sq. m. total exhibition space. Full specification: 70-page tech document by IDIRNET (Feb-Mar 2026).

37 Projectors
Barco I600 (4K+) on walls, G50 on floors. Ultra-short-throw lenses. Both rooms fully mapped. Room 1: 14 wall + 10 floor. Room 2: 8 wall + 5 floor.
87 Speakers (L-ISA)
L-Acoustics spatial audio across both rooms. 96 individually controlled sound objects in 3D space. 360-degree immersive sound. EUR 401,586.
5 Media Servers
Pixera PX2 Octo — professional immersive playback. Synchronized via precision clock (Meinberg). EUR 275,000.
28+ Sensors
Luxonis depth cameras with onboard AI processing. Real-time visitor tracking enables interactive experiences. EUR 32,116 (Room 1).

The Four Curatorial Principles

Non-negotiable foundations that govern all programming, communications, and design decisions.

Independence
Self-funded, self-directed, beholden to no agenda. External funding is welcome but never at the cost of editorial freedom.
Future Optimism
Technology as wonder, not dystopia. We show what's possible. We counter the prevailing doom narrative in contemporary art.
Awe
Design for collective wonder. Communal experiences where audiences feel profound awe together. Push technical boundaries constantly.
Pluralism
Everyone's story can be told. Ideas challenged through art, not censored. Focus on shared humanity and breadth of perspective.
