FAQ

Common questions

What is Lightheart?

Lightheart is Dublin's immersive gallery — a purpose-built space where large-scale projections, spatial audio, and curated art create experiences you feel in your body. Think of it as walking inside a painting, surrounded by sound that moves through the room.

How is this different from other "immersive experiences"?

Most immersive experiences in Dublin are entertainment-focused — projection-mapped rooms with popular imagery. Lightheart is a gallery. Our exhibitions are curated by a professional team, feature original and commissioned works, and change completely every quarter. The technology is world-class, but it serves the art — not the other way around.

How long does a visit take?

Each session is approximately 50 minutes. We recommend arriving 10 minutes before your session time.

Can I take photos?

Yes. Photography and video are encouraged — flash is not permitted. We want you to capture and share the experience.

Is it suitable for children?

Lightheart is suitable for ages 6 and above. Children under 14 must be accompanied by an adult. The experience is designed to inspire wonder across all ages.

Do exhibitions change?

Yes — every quarter (approximately every 12 weeks), the entire exhibition changes. New artists, new themes, new technology. Each visit is a new experience.

Can I host a private event?

Lightheart is available for corporate events, private viewings, and special occasions outside of public exhibition hours. Contact us at events@lightheart.ie for enquiries.

I have accessibility requirements.

Lightheart is fully wheelchair accessible with step-free entry. If you have specific accessibility needs, please contact us at access@lightheart.ie and we'll ensure your visit is comfortable.
