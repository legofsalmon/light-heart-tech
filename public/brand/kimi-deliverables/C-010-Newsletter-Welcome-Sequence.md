## EMAIL 2 — DAY 3
**Send:** 72 hours after Email 1  
**Purpose:** Founder story, emotional connection, build anticipation

### Subject Line Options
**Option A:** Why I decided to build this  
**Option B:** The space Dublin has been missing

### Preview Text
"Some things you can't stop thinking about until you build them."

### Body

Hi again,

I want to tell you why Lightheart exists.

Five years ago, I was in Tokyo. I spent an hour inside a teamLab installation — walls, floor, ceiling alive with digital life, sound moving through space, strangers standing together in silence, experiencing something none of us had words for.

I came back to Dublin and looked around. We have galleries that hang art on walls. We have theatres that stage performances. We have cinemas that show films.

What we don't have: a space where you stand inside the art. Where technology serves wonder. Where 50 minutes changes how you feel about what's possible.

So I'm building it.

Not because it's easy. Cultural infrastructure never is. But because the alternative — accepting that Irish artists will always leave to create this kind of work elsewhere — is unacceptable.

We open in September.

Between now and then:
- Construction completes (we're in final phase)
- Technical systems install (37 projectors, L-ISA spatial audio, real-time sensors)
- Artists begin creating work specifically for this space
- We announce our founding membership programme

If you want to see it come together, we share more frequent updates on Instagram: @lightheart.ie

Thanks for being here this early.

Brendan Dillon  
Founder

P.S. — Hit reply if you want to tell me what cultural space you think Dublin needs. I read every response.

## EMAIL 3 — DAY 7
**Send:** 7 days after Email 1 (4 days after Email 2)  
**Purpose:** Technical credibility, artist invitation, conversion to action

### Subject Line Options
**Option A:** What 37 projectors and 87 speakers make possible  
**Option B:** We built the instrument. Now we need the musicians.

### Preview Text
"This isn't a venue. It's infrastructure for something that doesn't exist yet."

### Body

Hi,

I want to show you what we're building.

Not the building. The infrastructure inside it.

37 Barco projectors. Positioned to the centimetre. Calibrated to work as a single canvas that covers every surface.

87 L-Acoustics speakers. L-ISA spatial audio — the same system Adele and Björk use — but configured for art. Sound that travels through space, not just from speakers.

44 depth-sensing cameras. Real-time processing. Five Pixera servers. Sub-3ms latency.

Translation: the environment responds to presence. It breathes with the room. It creates experiences that couldn't exist on a screen, in a headset, or anywhere else.

We've built the instrument. Now we're inviting artists to play it.

Lightheart doesn't rent space to artists. We commission work specifically for this infrastructure. We collaborate technically. IDIRNET — our technical partner — works with artists from concept through installation to create experiences that use every affordance of the system.

If you're an artist: we're reviewing proposals for 2027 exhibitions now. Our brief is at lightheart.ie/artists

If you're not an artist but you believe in this: our Founding Circle opens soon. 200 members, then it closes forever. Priority booking. Preview evenings. Permanent recognition.

Or simply tell someone who should know about Lightheart. Word of mouth is how cultural institutions grow.

Either way, thanks for reading this far. We'll be in touch when there's something worth sharing.

Brendan Dillon  
Founder, Lightheart

P.S. — One email a month, as promised. Next one comes when we have real news.

## AUTOMATION SETUP NOTES

### Timing
| Email | Trigger | Delay |
|-------|---------|-------|
| 1 | Form submission | Immediate |
| 2 | Email 1 sent | +72 hours |
| 3 | Email 1 sent | +7 days (or Email 2 +4 days) |

### Technical Requirements
- **Platform:** Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or similar
- **From address:** hello@lightheart.ie
- **Reply-to:** hello@lightheart.ie (Brendan reads replies)
- **Sender name:** Lightheart
- **Unsubscribe:** One-click, footer of every email

### Mobile Formatting
- Line breaks every 1-2 sentences
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- No dense blocks of text
- Single-column layout
- Minimum 16px font size

### Personalisation
- Use "Hi" or "Hello" (not "Hi [First Name]" unless data is clean)
- No merge tags beyond email address
- Keep it universal — works for everyone

### Testing Checklist
- [ ] Renders correctly in Gmail (mobile and desktop)
- [ ] Renders correctly in Apple Mail
- [ ] Renders correctly in Outlook
- [ ] Unsubscribe link works
- [ ] Reply-to address receives responses
- [ ] Subject lines don't trigger spam filters
- [ ] Preview text displays correctly

### A/B Test Setup (Subject Lines)
Split traffic 50/50 for Email 1:
- 50% receive Option A
- 50% receive Option B
- Measure: Open rate, click rate, unsubscribe rate
- Winner used for future subscribers after 100+ sends

## TONE & STYLE GUIDE (Applied)

✅ **Consistent elements:**
- Warm, direct, confident
- First person from Brendan
- Specific numbers (37, 87, 280)
- Short paragraphs for mobile
- Clear CTAs but not pushy
- Respect for subscriber's inbox

✅ **What we never do:**
- Exclamation marks (unprofessional)
- ALL CAPS (shouting)
- Urgency tactics ("limited time!")
- Corporate speak ("leverage," "synergy")
- Image-heavy (bandwidth, accessibility)

✅ **Sign-off consistency:**
- Always "Brendan Dillon"
- Always "Founder" or "Founder, Lightheart"
- Never just "Brendan" (too casual)
- Never "The Lightheart Team" (impersonal)
