## WHY 200?

### Section Headline
> Two hundred is not arbitrary.

### Body
We chose 200 with precision.

**Small enough to be real.**  
You will recognise other founding members at preview evenings. The artistic director will know your name within the first year. This is a community, not a mailing list.

**Large enough to matter.**  
200 people who each tell ten friends creates the groundswell that launches a cultural institution.

**Dunbar-adjacent.**  
200 sits within the range of community size where culture forms. Where inside jokes develop. Where being "one of the 200" means something.

**Operationally feasible.**  
We can write 200 welcome letters by hand. We can host 200 people for dinner. We cannot do this for 2,000.

**Financially significant.**  
200 founding members generate meaningful pre-opening revenue — and signal to stakeholders, press, and future visitors that Lightheart matters enough for 200 people to commit before the doors open.

When the 200th member joins, we close. The founding designation will never be available again.

## THE APPLICATION PROCESS

### Section Headline
> How to join the Founding Circle

### Step 1: Apply
Submit your application through the form below. Tell us which tier you are interested in and why you want to be part of Lightheart's founding community.

### Step 2: Review
We review applications in the order received. The Founding Circle is not about wealth — it is about commitment. We look for genuine enthusiasm, cultural engagement, and alignment with what Lightheart represents.

### Step 3: Welcome
If your application is accepted, you will receive:
- Welcome email from the artistic director
- Payment link (annual membership, founding price locked)
- Digital membership confirmation
- Estimated delivery date for your welcome pack

### Step 4: Receive
Within 14 days of joining:
- **Welcome pack** delivered to your address
- **Membership card** shipped (individually numbered)
- **Member portal access** activated
- **First newsletter** delivered

### Step 5: Belong
You are now one of the 200. Attend preview evenings. Bring guests. Introduce others. Your founding designation is permanent, regardless of future renewal decisions.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### What is your refund policy?

Founding Circle memberships can be cancelled within 14 days of purchase for a full refund, no questions asked. After 14 days, memberships are non-refundable but remain valid for the full year. The founding designation on our wall is permanent regardless of cancellation.

### Can I gift a membership?

Cornerstone members receive one transferable Circle membership to gift. For all other tiers, you may purchase a membership as a gift by applying in the recipient's name. The membership belongs to the individual, not transferable after activation (except the one-time relocation transfer; see below).

### Do you offer corporate memberships?

Corporate memberships are available through a separate programme designed for organisations. The Founding Circle is specifically for individuals making personal commitments. Corporate enquiries: corporate@lightheart.ie

### What if I live outside Dublin or Ireland?

International members are welcome. Your membership maintains your connection to Lightheart and provides access to exclusive digital content. If you relocate permanently outside Ireland, you may transfer your membership once to a Dublin-based friend or family member. Otherwise, your founding status remains permanent even if you cannot visit regularly.

### What happens after Year 1?

Founding Circle memberships are annual. If you maintain continuous membership, your founding price is locked permanently — you will always pay the rate you joined at. If you allow membership to lapse and later wish to rejoin, you may do so at the then-current rate (which will be higher). The founding designation on the wall remains permanent either way.

### Can I upgrade my tier?

Yes. You may upgrade at any time by paying the difference between your current tier and the new tier, subject to availability. If your desired tier is sold out, you will be placed on a priority waitlist for upgrades.

## WHY FOUNDING MEMBERSHIP IS DIFFERENT

### We Do Not Discount

Lightheart does not offer discounts. Not for students. Not for seniors. Not for groups. The price is the price because the value is the value.

Founding members do not receive a reduced rate. They receive **priority, access, and recognition** that general visitors will never have. The founding price is a commitment, not a bargain.

### This Is Investment, Not Purchase

You are not buying tickets in advance. You are investing in:
- **A cultural institution** that will exist for decades
- **A community** of 200 people who believe in the same thing
- **A legacy** — your name on the founding wall, permanently
- **An identity** — "I was there at the beginning"

### The Fortune, Not the Cookie

Every benefit has two layers. The cookie is what you get. The fortune is what it means.

| The Cookie | The Fortune |
|------------|-------------|
| Priority booking 48 hours early | You will never miss an exhibition. You will always be first. |
| Preview evening invitation | Standing in the room before anyone else. The privilege of the first impression. |
| Membership card | An object that reminds you who you are — someone who believed in this before it existed. |
| Name on the founding wall | Permanence. You are part of the building's story. |
| Curator walkthrough | Understanding the why behind the work. The difference between seeing and knowing. |
| Artist dinner | Sitting across from the person who made the work. A memory no ticket can buy. |

We lead with the fortune. The cookies are in the comparison table.
