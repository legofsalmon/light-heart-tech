## POST 7: THE MECHANISM — Speaker Array
**Pillar:** The Mechanism (3/3)  
**Timing:** Thursday, 7:30 PM (Thursday evening, planning weekend)

### Caption
87 speakers. 96 audio objects. 3.2 milliseconds latency.

This is L-ISA — the same spatial audio system used by Adele, Björk, and Beyoncé. But we're not doing concerts. We're doing art.

Sound won't come from "the speakers." It will travel through the room. Above you. Beside you. Through you. You'll hear things before you see them, and remember them long after you leave.

The physical infrastructure is invisible. Only the art remains.

## VOICE GUIDELINES (Applied Throughout)

✅ **We use:**
- First person plural ("we," "our")
- Technical specs as flex (37 projectors, 87 speakers, 3.2ms latency)
- Specific claims ("Room 1," "L-ISA," "Barco I600-4K10")
- Irish but cosmopolitan tone
- Confidence without arrogance
- "Surrounds you" not "immersive experience"

❌ **We never use:**
- "Immersive experience"
- "Step into"
- "Like nothing you've seen before"
- Corporate buzzwords
- Vague superlatives
- Discount language
