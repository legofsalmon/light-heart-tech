## 1. THE OPPORTUNITY

### Lightheart: Ireland's First Purpose-Built Immersive Gallery

Opening September 2026 on Dublin's quays, Lightheart represents a €4–4.5 million investment in permanent cultural infrastructure. This is not a temporary exhibition or adapted warehouse. It is a purpose-built 280-square-metre venue designed specifically for immersive art — with 37 permanent projectors, 87 L-ISA spatial audio speakers, and real-time interactive systems.

**What We Do**

Lightheart commissions ambitious immersive art from international and Irish artists, presented in quarterly exhibitions that transform the space completely every twelve weeks. Each exhibition is created specifically for our infrastructure — work that could not exist in any other venue.

**Scale & Impact**

- **Year 1 visitor target:** 150,000–200,000
- **Reach:** Dublin residents, Irish domestic visitors, international tourists (particularly UK, Europe, North America)
- **Founding community:** 200 founding members pre-launch
- **Infrastructure permanence:** Built to operate for decades

**Why Now**

Dublin has extraordinary galleries, theatres, and museums. What it lacks is a permanent venue for immersive art — the form that dominates contemporary cultural conversation globally. Lightheart fills that gap. Partners who join us at launch become part of establishing Ireland's presence in this field.

## 2. WHY PARTNER

### Audience

Lightheart audiences are culturally engaged, experience-seeking, and professionally influential:

- **Age:** Primarily 25–55
- **Location:** 60% Dublin/local, 40% domestic Irish/international tourist
- **Profile:** Creative professionals, technology workers, arts attenders, experience-seekers, cultural tourists
- **Behaviour:** High social sharing, return visitation, word-of-mouth amplification

They are not passive consumers. They are active participants who value quality, authenticity, and cultural depth. They notice who supports the arts they care about.

### Brand Alignment

Partnership with Lightheart signals:

- **Cultural credibility:** Association with ambitious, permanent cultural infrastructure
- **Innovation:** Connection to technology-forward artistic practice
- **Place:** Investment in Dublin's cultural landscape
- **Quality:** Alignment with carefully crafted experiences over mass entertainment

This is not logo placement on a banner. It is meaningful association with a significant cultural institution at its inception.

### Visibility & Recognition

Partners are acknowledged across:
- On-site presence in the gallery
- Digital channels (website, social media, newsletters)
- Press and media coverage
- Private partner events and hospitality

Recognition is proportional to partnership level and tailored to partner objectives.

## 3. PARTNERSHIP TIERS

We offer four partnership structures, each designed for different organisational objectives and capacities. All partnerships are negotiable within these frameworks.

### Exhibition Partner

**The Opportunity:** Title sponsorship of a quarterly exhibition

Exhibition Partners are the presenting sponsor of one of Lightheart's four annual exhibitions. Your organisation is named in the exhibition title: "[Organisation Name] presents [Exhibition Name] by [Artist]."

**Investment:** From €[TBD — suggested range €50,000–€100,000 per exhibition]

**What This Supports:**
- Artist commissioning fee
- Technical production costs
- Marketing and press campaign
- Educational programming

**Duration:** 12-week exhibition run, with acknowledgment in archive and legacy materials permanently

**Ideal For:** Brands seeking significant visibility, arts organisations extending reach, companies with cultural alignment

---

### Technology Partner

**The Opportunity:** Partnership on technical infrastructure or innovation

Technology Partners contribute equipment, expertise, or funding specifically for Lightheart's technical capabilities. This could include projection systems, audio equipment, network infrastructure, software, or technical development.

**Investment:** In-kind equipment, technical services, or financial contribution equivalent

**What This Supports:**
- Technical infrastructure acquisition or upgrade
- Innovation projects (new capabilities, experimental systems)
- Technical education and documentation

**Duration:** Varies by contribution; minimum 12-month acknowledgment, longer-term for significant infrastructure

**Ideal For:** AV manufacturers, technology companies, innovation-focused organisations, research institutions

---

### Community Partner

**The Opportunity:** Support for accessibility, education, or outreach programmes

Community Partners fund specific programmes that extend Lightheart's reach: relaxed sessions for neurodivergent visitors, audio description services, schools programming, community group access, or artist development initiatives.

**Investment:** From €[TBD — suggested range €10,000–€30,000 per programme]

**What This Supports:**
- Programme delivery and staffing
- Accessibility services and equipment
- Transportation and access costs for community groups
- Free or subsidised tickets for underserved audiences

**Duration:** Annual programme cycle, with ongoing acknowledgment

**Ideal For:** Foundations, CSR programmes, community-focused organisations, funders with social impact objectives

---

### Founding Supporter

**The Opportunity:** Pre-opening partnership with permanent acknowledgment

Founding Supporters join Lightheart before the doors open. Your support is acknowledged permanently on our founding wall — the physical record of those who made this possible — and in all founding documentation.

**Investment:** From €[TBD — suggested range €25,000–€75,000]

**What This Supports:**
- Pre-opening operations and setup
- Founding member community building
- Initial programming and commissioning

**Duration:** Permanent acknowledgment on founding wall and in institutional history

**Ideal For:** Organisations committed to Dublin's cultural infrastructure, long-term partners, those who value legacy association
