## What Makes Lightheart Different

### A Venue That Transforms

**Projection-Mapped Architecture**  
Every surface in Lightheart can display custom content. Your branding. Your vision. Your story — mapped across 280 square metres of wall, floor, and ceiling. Our technical team works with you to create environments impossible in conventional venues.

**L-ISA Spatial Audio**  
Sound moves through the space, not just from speakers. Presentations gain dimension. Ambient soundscapes create atmosphere. Music surrounds your guests. This is the same spatial audio system used in major concert tours — configured for intimate, powerful experiences.

**Two Distinct Galleries**  
- **Room 1:** 180 sq. m. — Grand scale for major events, presentations, and celebrations
- **Room 2:** 100 sq. m. — Intimate setting for dinners, meetings, and focused gatherings

Hire separately or together. Each space maintains Lightheart's immersive capabilities.

**Lightheart Cafe**  
Our in-house cafe provides catering partners with dedicated preparation space and service infrastructure. From canapés to multi-course dinners, the food service matches the venue's ambition.

## Enquiry Process

### Step 1: Initial Enquiry
Email events@lightheart.ie with:
- Proposed date(s) and time
- Event type and brief description
- Expected guest numbers
- Room preference (Room 1, Room 2, or both)
- Any specific technical requirements
- Budget range (optional, helps us recommend appropriately)

### Step 2: Consultation
Our events team responds within 2 working days to:
- Confirm availability (or suggest alternatives)
- Discuss your vision and requirements
- Provide indicative pricing
- Arrange venue visit if desired

### Step 3: Proposal
You receive a detailed proposal including:
- Venue hire costs
- Technical specification
- Timeline and logistics
- Terms and conditions
- Booking confirmation process

### Step 4: Confirmation
Secure your date by:
- Returning signed agreement
- Payment of deposit (50% of venue hire)
- Confirmation of final numbers (14 days before event)

### Step 5: Event Delivery
- Technical rehearsal (scheduled in advance)
- Event day delivery
- Post-event debrief and final invoice

**Advance Booking:** Minimum 8 weeks for standard events; 12 weeks for complex technical requirements
