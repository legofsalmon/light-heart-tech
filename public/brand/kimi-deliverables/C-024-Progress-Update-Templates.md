# Construction & Fit-Out Progress Updates

**Document Type:** Template + Example updates for stakeholders  
**Audience:** Investors, Founding Circle members, internal team  
**Status:** Active (March–September 2026)  
**Last Updated:** March 2026

---

## TEMPLATE STRUCTURE

Use this structure for all progress updates. Adjust tone slightly by audience:
- **Investors:** Emphasise budget control, timeline adherence, risk management
- **Founding Circle:** Emphasise what's being built for them, exclusivity, opening date
- **Internal team:** Include operational detail, decision points, dependencies

```markdown
**PERIOD COVERED:** [Month Year]

**KEY MILESTONES COMPLETED**
- [Specific achievement with numbers/dates where possible]
- [Specific achievement]
- [Specific achievement]

**KEY MILESTONES UPCOMING**
- [Specific upcoming task with target date]
- [Specific upcoming task with target date]

**CRITICAL PATH STATUS:** [On track / At risk / Delayed — be specific about risk factors]

**PHOTO/VISUAL SUGGESTION:** [What photo or visual would accompany this update]

**BUDGET NOTE (Internal only):** [Variance, concerns, or confirmation — omit for external audiences]

**NEXT UPDATE:** [Date]
```

## TONE GUIDANCE BY AUDIENCE

### For Investors
- Lead with timeline adherence and risk status
- Include budget variance in every update
- Use precise numbers (percentages, dates, financials)
- Acknowledge problems early; don't bury bad news

### For Founding Circle Members
- Emphasise what they're getting (exclusivity, priority access, their name on the wall)
- Use "your" and "we're building this for you"
- Include aspirational detail (artist names, exhibition titles)
- Keep technical detail light but specific enough to sound credible

### For Internal Team
- Include operational dependencies (who needs what from whom)
- Note decision points requiring input
- Flag blockers explicitly
- Use technical terminology freely (team understands it)

---

## CRITICAL PATH DEFINITIONS

Use these terms consistently:

| Status | Meaning | Example language |
|--------|---------|------------------|
| **On track** | Timeline and deliverables align with plan | "All milestones achieved as scheduled" |
| **At risk** | Potential delay or issue identified but manageable | "External dependency (fire cert) could delay start by 1–2 weeks if not received by April 22" |
| **Delayed** | Milestone missed or timeline slipped | "Acoustic fabric delivery delayed 10 days due to supplier issue. Gallery 2 treatment pushed to June 8" |

---

## PHOTO GUIDELINES

- **Pre-construction:** Floor plans, equipment specs, site survey shots
- **Construction:** Wide angles showing scale, specific detail shots (mounts, cable runs)
- **Final month:** Atmospheric shots, team at work, building exterior
- **Never show:** Unfinished spaces without context, messy construction detail, personnel without consent
