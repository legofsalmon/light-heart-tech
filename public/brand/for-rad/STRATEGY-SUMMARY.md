## The Project

**Lightheart** is a purpose-built immersive gallery on Dublin's quays (near Guinness Storehouse). Quarterly changing exhibitions using 14 ultra-short-throw 4K laser projectors across walls and floors, with L-ISA spatial audio (37-50 speakers, 360-degree sound). Total investment approximately EUR 4-4.5m. Opening September 2026.

**IDIRNET** (Kev Freeney's studio) handles creative direction, technical design, and AV systems via B2B retainer.

---

## Core Strategic Mantra

**Build credibility first. Build clarity second. Build noise last.**

This governs the entire rollout: website first, personal intros second, social third, press fourth, paid last.

---

## Brand Voice (5 Tensions)

| Be This | Not This |
|---------|----------|
| Confident | Arrogant |
| Technical | Inaccessible |
| Irish | Parochial |
| Warm | Twee |
| Institutional | Corporate |

**Reference point:** Think European cultural institution (Tate Modern's confidence), not Dublin nightlife venue or WeWork friendliness.

**Design direction (agreed Mar 9):** European/Swiss style. Timeless, not trendy. Striking, bold, simple. Typography is the critical connective thread. The brand system must flex between "arty/experimental" and "family-friendly/accessible" — same system, different register.

---

## 4 Curatorial Principles (Non-Negotiable)

Every piece of content, campaign, and design decision should be testable against these:

| Principle | Meaning | Comms Implication |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| **Independence** | Self-funded, self-directed, beholden to no agenda | Signal conviction without being combative |
| **Future Optimism** | Technology as wonder, not dystopia | Energising, forward-looking tone. Avoid catastrophism |
| **Awe** | Collective wonder over individual spectacle | Visual content must convey scale. BTS builds credibility |
| **Pluralism** | Everyone's story can be told | Emphasise breadth of perspective. Don't shy from provocative work |

## Two-Layer Positioning

| Layer | Where | Message |
|-------|-------|---------|
| **Layer 1 (Core)** | Homepage, tickets, social | A world-class immersive gallery. Quarterly exhibitions. Ticketed cultural destination. Clear visitor promise. |
| **Layer 2 (Ambition)** | About page, press, institutional | Community infrastructure for digital art. Education. R&D. Artist development. |

**Rule:** Layer 2 enriches Layer 1 but never replaces it.

## Three Audiences

All campaigns must serve at least one:

| Audience | Message | Primary Channels |
|----------|---------|-----------------|
| **General Public** (experience-seekers) | A new kind of cultural experience in Dublin | Instagram, Website, Tourism |
| **Creative Community** (artists, designers) | A serious space for serious work | LinkedIn, direct outreach, artist networks |
| **Institutions** (education, funders, tourism) | Infrastructure that raises Ireland's cultural capacity | LinkedIn, direct email, governance page |

## What Lightheart Is NOT

- Not a selfie museum or Instagram trap
- Not a one-room optical gimmick
- Not a tourist trap
- Not trend-driven
- Not a 5-channel social blitz from day one

**Key differentiator:** Quarterly changeover programming. Unlike illusion galleries and touring shows that never change, Lightheart is always new.

## Founding Circle (Membership Programme)

200 founding members total (hard cap). Three tiers:

| Tier | Price | Available | Profile |
|------|-------|-----------|---------|
| **Circle** | EUR 199/year | 120 | Culturally engaged Dubliners, wide social networks |
| **Inner Circle** | EUR 499/year | 60 | Professionals, 30s-50s, value culture and exclusivity |
| **Cornerstone** | EUR 999/year | 20 | Patrons, collectors, business leaders |

RAD to design: membership cards (tier-specific treatments), welcome pack materials.

**Rule:** Lightheart does not discount, ever.
